WELCOME TO STOCK-CRYPTO-APP

The purpose of the STOCK-CRYPTO-APP recommendation system is to leverage state-of-the-art transformer-based language models to provide users with personalized investment recommendations for both stocks and cryptocurrencies. By analyzing vast amounts of data and user preferences, it aims to assist investors in making informed financial decisions, optimizing their portfolios, and maximizing returns
